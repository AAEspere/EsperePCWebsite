Welcome

Welcome to PC Parts Explained! This website is intended for people who are interested in building their first PC, but lack the technical knowledege to feel confident in doing so. I will be explaining in laymans terms the functionality of the part, and how it will affect the whole PC. I will also provide my perspective on the individual part: how important I deem a part and how much I recommend spending on it, current popular parts, and will give some sample builds!

I will be going over the following parts in detail:
- The CPU
- The Motherboard
- The RAM
- The GPU
- The SDD and HDD
- and the PSU

PLEASE NOTE I will be providing my own perspective on each part of the PC, but that doesn't mean my word should be taken as law! I want this website to be a hub of information for beginner PC builders to reference, but this website should be used to learn the basics of PC parts. I recommend extending your knowledege of PCs by doing extended research!
